Creamy Pasta Pesto

Time required: 15 Mins

Recipie Description

A quick pasta recipe that has an aromatic taste with fresh
and toasted pine nuts blended with a tangy pesto sauce.
Carefully prepared to ensure your family's happiness with
a rich blend of Puck Cooking Cream, spices, and parmesan cheese
prepared within a few minutes

    Ingredients

    1 (16 ounce) package pasta
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    ½ cup chopped onion
    2 ½ tablespoons pesto
    salt to taste
    ground black pepper to taste
    2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
    Directions
  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water
  2. Stir in pasta and return to a boil.
  3. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  5. heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes.
  6. Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.
  7. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.
